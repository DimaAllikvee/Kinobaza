Cloud Atlas

Rating: 7.4 / 10
Year: 2012
Directors: Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski, Tom Tykwer

Description

"Cloud Atlas" is a film intertwining multiple stories set in different eras but connected by a common theme: how the actions of individuals can impact the past, present, and future. The film explores ideas of reincarnation, free will, and the struggle for independence in a world full of oppression.
