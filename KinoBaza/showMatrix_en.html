The Matrix

Rating: 8.0 / 10
Year: 1999
Directors: Lana and Lilly Wachowski

Description

In the movie The Matrix, the main character, Neo, discovers that the world he lives in is a virtual simulation created to hide the real reality where humanity is enslaved by machines. Neo must decide which reality to choose - the one he lives in or the real one, full of danger and struggle for freedom.
